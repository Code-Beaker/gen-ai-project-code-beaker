FAQ

What is GenAI?
GenAI is an intelligent chatbot designed to help answer your questions, provide recommendations, and assist with various tasks. Powered by advanced AI, it offers natural and engaging conversations tailored to your needs.
How does GenAI work?
GenAI uses natural language processing (NLP) to understand your input and generate accurate responses. It learns from vast amounts of data and is constantly improving its ability to provide helpful and context-aware answers.
What kind of tasks can GenAI help with?
GenAI can assist with a wide range of tasks, including answering questions, offering advice, providing technical support, generating ideas, and even helping with creative writing or decision-making processes.
Can GenAI replace human assistance?
GenAI is a powerful tool that can assist with many tasks, but it is not meant to replace human assistance. It works best when used as a complementary resource for obtaining information and guidance.
Is GenAI safe to use?
Yes, GenAI is designed with safety and privacy in mind. It does not store personal conversations, and all interactions are secured to ensure user confidentiality and safety.
